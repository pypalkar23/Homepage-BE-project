LET'S PLAY

Getting Started

Step 1

Visit the url iceskirun.appspot.com from both the devices(laptop/desktop and mobile) and click on Let's Play button.

Step 2

From your desktop click on "Generate Room" button to generate four digit room number and then press "Create Room" to create room for your devices on the server.

Step 3

Now enter the same room number on your mobile device's text box and click on "Join room".Then wait for two devices to connect.

Step 4

As soon as the connection is established desktop page will show the "Let's Play" button.Click on that to kick off things.

Play the game

Now Desktop/laptop will display the game arena and mobile device will display the game controller UI. Hold the mobile device in the landscape orientation and control the movement of the game character using the accelerometer of your mobile device.
